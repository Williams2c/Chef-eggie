Home Delivery

In an effort to overcome the challenges in most modern cities for people to have breakfast, lunch or supper in the comfort of their homes before going to their offices, schools or even when the get home, Chef Eggie's Kitchen offers a wide array of quality product offerings for Home Delivery. This includes our continental meals like Chips (Fries), Fried rice, Coconut rice, Chicken (different types), Fishes (Spicy, “In-sauce”, Fillet etc). Our Africana brand offers a very wide range of delicious African soups like Ogbono, Egusi, Efori-iro, Edikaing-kong etc. We also offer Swallows like Eba, Pounded Yam, Amala, Semovita etc. You also have the opportunity of having our Porridge Yam, Pottage Beans, Ofada Rice, Plantain, Stock Fish, Cowleg, Moin-Moin etc at affordable prices.
